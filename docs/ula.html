And some final notes.

  • Consonant followed by "h" is aspiration.
  • Accents are breves on vowels: ȃ, ȇ, ȋ, ȏ, ȗ.
  • Rising waves (tones) are: á, é, í, ó, ú.
  • Falling waves are: à, è, ì, ò, ù.
  • High waves are: ā, ē, ī, ō, ū.
  • Low waves are: a̱, e̱, i̱, o̱, u̱.
  • Falling/rising waves are: ǎ, ě, ǐ, ǒ, ǔ.
  • Clicks are circumflex on consonants.
  • Trill is double consonant.
  • Implosives are backtick on consonant.
  • Explosives are forward tick on consonants.
  • All glottal stops are '.
